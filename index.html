Short Bio Jeroen Riemsdijk

Jeroen Riemsdijk is concert pianist, chamber musician, teacher and author. He performs regularly as soloist and with distinguished artists in Concerts Halls, on Festivals and in the recording studio. Riemsdijk teaches piano at the Piano Faculty at the Conservatoire of Maastricht. His discography contains music by Shostakovich, Zwaag, Rääts, Kalkbrenner, Debussy and many others. Jeroen Riemsdijk is autor of 'The Golden Fingers Technique. The art of Passagework for Pianists.
